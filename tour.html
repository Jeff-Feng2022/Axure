View and organize widgets in the Outline

View a list of all widgets on the current page in the Outline pane.


The Outline allows you to view a widget's z-index position. By default, the Outline is ordered front-to-back, so widgets at the top are in front, and widgets at the bottom are at the back.

Group widgets and define Resizing Constraints

Group multiple widgets via the main toolbar or using Ctrl + G (Win) or Cmd + G (Mac) when you'd like to treat them as one when for editing, moving, or applying interactions.

Group

Ungroup

Once grouped, you can define Resizing Constraints for widgets within the group to lock a widget's absolute distance from an edge of the group, or the vertical or horizontal center of the group.


When resizing a group, any widgets with constraints applied to them will always maintain their distance, depending on the constraints chosen.


You can lock a widget's width or height when a group is resized using Fix Width or Fix Height.


Resizing Constraints can also be applied to widgets within Dynamic Panels.
